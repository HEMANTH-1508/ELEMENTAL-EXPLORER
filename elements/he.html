He

Helium

  • Atomic Number: 2
  • Atomic Weight: 4.0026
  • Discovered By: Pierre Janssen & Joseph Norman Lockyer (1868)
  • State at Room Temperature: Gas
  • Uses: Cryogenics, medical applications, cooling in MRI machines, balloons, and airships.
  • Melting Point: -272.2 °C
  • Boiling Point: -268.93 °C
  • Electronegativity: Not applicable (Helium is a noble gas)
  • Group: 18 (Noble gases)
