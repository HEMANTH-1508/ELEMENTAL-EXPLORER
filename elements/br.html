Bromine

  • Atomic Number: 35
  • Atomic Weight: 79.904
  • Discovered By: Antoine Jérôme Balard (1826)
  • State at Room Temperature: Liquid
  • Uses: Flame retardants, water purification, and as a disinfectant.
  • Melting Point: -7.2 °C
  • Boiling Point: 58.8 °C
  • Electronegativity: 2.96
  • Group: 17 (Halogens)
