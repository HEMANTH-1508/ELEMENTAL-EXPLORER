Lanthanoids

La

Lanthanum

Atomic Number: 57

Atomic Mass: 138.90547 u

Lanthanum is a key component in high-quality optical glasses and catalysts.

Ce

Cerium

Atomic Number: 58

Atomic Mass: 140.116 u

Cerium is used in catalytic converters, glass polishing, and as an alloying agent.

Pr

Praseodymium

Atomic Number: 59

Atomic Mass: 140.90766 u

Praseodymium is used in aircraft engines and in creating strong magnets.

Nd

Neodymium

Atomic Number: 60

Atomic Mass: 144.242 u

Neodymium is essential for making powerful magnets used in electronics and motors.

Pm

Promethium

Atomic Number: 61

Atomic Mass: 145 u

Promethium is a rare radioactive element used in nuclear batteries and luminescent paints.

Sm

Samarium

Atomic Number: 62

Atomic Mass: 150.36 u

Samarium is used in magnets, lasers, and as a neutron absorber in nuclear reactors.

Eu

Europium

Atomic Number: 63

Atomic Mass: 151.964 u

Europium is widely used in phosphorescent and fluorescent applications, including TV screens.

Gd

Gadolinium

Atomic Number: 64

Atomic Mass: 157.25 u

Gadolinium is used in MRI contrast agents and as a neutron absorber in nuclear reactors.

Tb

Terbium

Atomic Number: 65

Atomic Mass: 158.92535 u

Terbium is used in solid-state devices and as a green phosphor in electronics.

Dy

Dysprosium

Atomic Number: 66

Atomic Mass: 162.5 u

Dysprosium is used in high-performance magnets, lasers, and nuclear reactors.

Ho

Holmium

Atomic Number: 67

Atomic Mass: 164.93033 u

Holmium is used in nuclear reactors as a burnable poison and in solid-state lasers.

Er

Erbium

Atomic Number: 68

Atomic Mass: 167.259 u

Erbium is used in fiber optics, especially in telecommunications, and in lasers.

Tm

Thulium

Atomic Number: 69

Atomic Mass: 168.93422 u

Thulium is used in portable X-ray devices and in laser technology.

Yb

Ytterbium

Atomic Number: 70

Atomic Mass: 173.045 u

Ytterbium is used in stress gauges, lasers, and as a doping agent in certain steel alloys.

Lu

Lutetium

Atomic Number: 71

Atomic Mass: 174.9668 u

Lutetium is used in PET scan detectors, and as a catalyst in petroleum refining.
