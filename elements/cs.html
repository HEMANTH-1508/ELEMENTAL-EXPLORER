Caesium

  • Atomic Number: 55
  • Atomic Weight: 132.91
  • Discovered By: Robert Bunsen, Gustav Kirchhoff (1860)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Atomic clocks, photoelectric cells, and in radiation therapy.
  • Melting Point: 28.44 °C
  • Boiling Point: 671 °C
  • Electronegativity: 0.79
  • Group: 1 (Alkali Metals)
