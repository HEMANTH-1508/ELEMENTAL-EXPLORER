Radium

  • Atomic Number: 88
  • Atomic Weight: 226
  • Discovered By: Marie Curie, Pierre Curie, and André-Louis Debierne (1898)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Formerly used in luminescent paint, medical treatments for cancer.
  • Melting Point: 700 °C
  • Boiling Point: 1413 °C
  • Electronegativity: 0.9
  • Group: 2 (Alkaline Earth Metals)
