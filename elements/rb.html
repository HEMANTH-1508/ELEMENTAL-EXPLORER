Rubidium

  • Atomic Number: 37
  • Atomic Weight: 85.468
  • Discovered By: Robert Bunsen, Gustav Kirchhoff (1861)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Research, atomic clocks, and energy storage systems.
  • Melting Point: 39.3 °C
  • Boiling Point: 688 °C
  • Electronegativity: 0.82
  • Group: 1 (Alkali Metals)
