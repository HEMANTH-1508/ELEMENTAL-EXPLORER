  • Atomic Number: 75
  • Atomic Weight: 186.21
  • Discovered By: Ida Noddack, Otto Berg (1925)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Jet engine turbines, electrical contacts, and catalysts in petroleum refining.
  • Melting Point: 3186 °C
  • Boiling Point: 5596 °C
  • Electronegativity: 2.2
  • Group: 7 (Transition Metals)
