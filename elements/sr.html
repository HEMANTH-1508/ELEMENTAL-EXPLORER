Sr

Strontium

  • Atomic Number: 38
  • Atomic Weight: 87.62
  • Discovered By: William Cruickshank and Humphry Davy (1808)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Fireworks, red pigments, magnets, and in the production of ferrite magnets.
  • Melting Point: 777 °C
  • Boiling Point: 1382 °C
  • Electronegativity: 0.95
  • Group: 2 (Alkaline Earth Metals)
