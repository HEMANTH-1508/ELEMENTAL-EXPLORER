Argon

  • Atomic Number: 18
  • Atomic Weight: 39.948
  • Discovered By: Lord Rayleigh and William Ramsay (1894)
  • State at Room Temperature: Gas
  • Uses: Lighting (neon signs), inert atmosphere in welding, cryogenics.
  • Melting Point: -189.34 °C
  • Boiling Point: -185.85 °C
  • Electronegativity: Not applicable (noble gas)
  • Group: 18 (Noble Gases)
