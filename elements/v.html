Vanadium

  • Atomic Number: 23
  • Atomic Weight: 50.941
  • Discovered By: Andrés Manuel del Río (1801)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Alloying agent in steel, manufacturing of batteries, catalysts in chemical reactions.
  • Melting Point: 1910 °C
  • Boiling Point: 3380 °C
  • Electronegativity: 1.63
  • Group: 5 (Transition Metals)
