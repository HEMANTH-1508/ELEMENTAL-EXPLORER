  • Atomic Number: 15
  • Atomic Weight: 30.974
  • Discovered By: Hennig Brand (1669)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Fertilizers, detergents, flame retardants, pesticides.
  • Melting Point: 44.1 °C (white phosphorus)
  • Boiling Point: 280.5 °C (white phosphorus)
  • Electronegativity: 2.19
  • Group: 15 (Nonmetals)
