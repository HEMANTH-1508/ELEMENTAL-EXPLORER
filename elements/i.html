Iodine

  • Atomic Number: 53
  • Atomic Weight: 126.90
  • Discovered By: Bernard Courtois (1811)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Disinfectants, thyroid medications, and antiseptics.
  • Melting Point: 113.7 °C
  • Boiling Point: 184.3 °C
  • Electronegativity: 2.66
  • Group: 17 (Halogens)
