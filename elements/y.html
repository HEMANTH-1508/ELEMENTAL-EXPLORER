Yttrium

  • Atomic Number: 39
  • Atomic Weight: 88.906
  • Discovered By: Johan Gadolin (1794)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Phosphors in color television tubes, superconductors, and in nuclear reactors.
  • Melting Point: 1526 °C
  • Boiling Point: 3337 °C
  • Electronegativity: 1.22
  • Group: 3 (Transition Metals)
