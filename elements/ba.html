Barium

  • Atomic Number: 56
  • Atomic Weight: 137.33
  • Discovered By: Humphry Davy (1808)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Drilling fluids, fireworks, and as a contrast medium in medical imaging.
  • Melting Point: 725 °C
  • Boiling Point: 1640 °C
  • Electronegativity: 0.89
  • Group: 2 (Alkaline Earth Metals)
