Technetium

  • Atomic Number: 43
  • Atomic Weight: 98
  • Discovered By: Carlo Perrier, Emilio Segrè (1937)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Medical imaging, radiopharmaceuticals, and research in nuclear physics.
  • Melting Point: 2157 °C
  • Boiling Point: 4265 °C
  • Electronegativity: 1.9
  • Group: 7 (Transition Metals)
