Astatine

  • Atomic Number: 85
  • Atomic Weight: 210
  • Discovered By: Dale R. Corson, Ronald A. Meyer, and Glenn T. Seaborg (1940)
  • State at Room Temperature: Solid
  • Uses: Limited research applications in medicine and radiation therapy.
  • Melting Point: 302 °C
  • Boiling Point: 337 °C
  • Electronegativity: 2.2
  • Group: 17 (Halogens)
