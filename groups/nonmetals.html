Non-Metals

Non-metals are elements that generally lack the properties of metals. They are poor conductors of heat and electricity and are typically brittle in solid form. Non-metals include both gases and solids, and they tend to have higher electronegativity compared to metals. The non-metals include:

  • Hydrogen (H)
  • Carbon (C)
  • Nitrogen (N)
  • Oxygen (O)
  • Fluorine (F)
  • Chlorine (Cl)
  • Bromine (Br)
  • Iodine (I)
  • Phosphorus (P)
  • Sulfur (S)
  • Selenium (Se)
  • Argon (Ar)

Characteristics of Non-Metals

Non-metals exhibit the following properties:

  • Good insulators (poor conductors of heat and electricity).
  • Brittle in solid form and cannot be hammered into sheets or drawn into wires.
  • Have high electronegativity values, meaning they tend to attract electrons in chemical reactions.
  • Non-metals form acidic oxides that dissolve in water to form acids.
  • They have low melting and boiling points compared to metals.

Applications of Non-Metals

  • Hydrogen: Used in fuel cells, hydrogenation of oils, and as rocket fuel.
  • Carbon: Essential in all life forms, used in steel manufacturing, and as a source of energy in the form of coal, oil, and natural gas.
  • Nitrogen: Used in fertilizers, explosives, and in the production of ammonia.
  • Oxygen: Essential for respiration, used in medical applications and in steel manufacturing.
  • Fluorine: Used in the production of Teflon, and in toothpaste as a fluoride compound.
  • Chlorine: Used in disinfectants, PVC production, and in bleach.
  • Sulfur: Used in the production of sulfuric acid, fertilizers, and in the vulcanization of rubber.
  • Iodine: Used in medical disinfectants, in iodine salts, and in the production of certain dyes.

Interesting Facts

  • Oxygen makes up about 21% of the Earth's atmosphere and is vital for respiration in humans and animals.
  • Carbon is found in all living organisms and is a key component of organic molecules.
  • Chlorine is widely used as a disinfectant, particularly in swimming pools and drinking water treatment.
